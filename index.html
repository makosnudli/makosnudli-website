About Mákos Nudli

Mákos Nudli is a modern bakery and café concept inspired by Hungarian flavours, artisan baking and everyday coffee culture. Our aim is to create a place in Luleå where guests can enjoy both familiar comfort and something unique.

What we serve

A carefully selected menu built around pastries, desserts and coffee.

Hungarian favourites

Mákos nudli, diós nudli, seasonal sweet specialties and traditional desserts.

Bakery selection

Fresh pastries, buns, breads and baked goods prepared for daily café service.

Coffee

Espresso-based drinks, filter coffee and carefully selected beans for a premium café experience.
